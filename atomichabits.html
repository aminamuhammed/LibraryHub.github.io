Atomic Habits

Atomic Habits by James Clear is a practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones through small, incremental changes. The book emphasizes that by making tiny adjustments—what Clear calls "atomic habits"—you can achieve remarkable results over time. Clear provides actionable strategies based on cutting-edge psychology and neuroscience, making it easier to implement lasting behavior changes.

In addition to the step-by-step methods, Clear also delves into the science of habit formation, explaining how habits work and why they are so powerful. He highlights the importance of focusing on systems rather than goals, encouraging readers to make consistent, small improvements that compound into significant personal and professional growth.
